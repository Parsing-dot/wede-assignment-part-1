Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our enterprise ICT solutions.
Hover over highlighted terms to learn more.

What is Dedicated Internet Access (DIA)?

Dedicated Internet Access is a premium internet service that provides exclusive, uncontended bandwidth reserved solely for your business. Unlike shared connections, DIA guarantees consistent speeds, 99.9% uptime, and symmetrical upload/download rates.

How long does installation take?

Installation timelines vary based on the service type and location. Standard fibre installations typically take 5-10 business days, while wireless solutions can be deployed within 3-5 business days. Complex enterprise installations may require 2-4 weeks.

Do you offer SLA guarantees?

Yes, all our business solutions come with comprehensive Service Level Agreements guaranteeing 99.9% uptime, 24/7 network monitoring, and rapid response times. Enterprise clients receive priority support with 4-hour repair SLAs.

What areas do you cover?

We primarily serve the Greater Johannesburg area, including Soweto and surrounding regions. Our wireless network covers most metropolitan areas, and we're continuously expanding our fibre footprint. Contact us to check availability at your specific location.

Is the internet really uncapped and unshaped?

Yes! Our dedicated internet solutions are truly uncapped (no data limits) and unshaped (no traffic throttling). You get the full speed you pay for, 24/7, with no restrictions on usage or content.

What support do you provide via your NOC?

We offer 24/7 technical support through our Network Operations Centre. Our expert team monitors your connection around the clock and provides proactive maintenance, troubleshooting, and rapid incident response.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! Our solutions are scalable. You can upgrade your bandwidth or add services as your business grows without major infrastructure changes. Contact our team to discuss upgrade options.
