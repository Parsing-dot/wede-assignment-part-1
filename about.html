Our Story

Empowering South African enterprises with reliable, high-speed connectivity since 2006.

Who We Are

Mdukazi Projects is a leading provider of Enterprise ITC Solutions, specializing in Dedicated Internet Access (DIA) for businesses of all sizes. Established in 2006 as Khulacom Communications, we have grown into a comprehensive ICT solutions provider serving individuals and organisations across South Africa.

Our mission is to empower South African enterprises with uncapped, unshaped, and guaranteed high-speed connectivity that drives digital transformation and business growth.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, scalable, and transparent connectivity solutions that enable businesses to thrive in the digital age.

Our Vision

To be South Africa's most trusted partner in enterprise ICT infrastructure, bridging the digital divide with innovation and excellence.

Our Promise

Fixed pricing, guaranteed uptime, and 24/7 expert support. No hidden fees, no throttling, just premium connectivity.

Our Journey

  • 2006: Founded as Khulacom Communications to bridge the connectivity gap for South African enterprises.
  • 2012: Expanded network coverage to major metropolitan areas, serving over 500 businesses.
  • 2018: Achieved 99.9% uptime guarantee with comprehensive SLA-backed support systems.
  • 2024: Rebranded to Mdukazi Projects, investing in next-gen fiber and wireless infrastructure.
