Who We Are

Mdukazi Projects is a leading provider of Enterprise ITC Solutions, specializing in Dedicated Internet Access (DIA) for businesses of all sizes. Established in 2006 as Khulacom Communications, we have grown into a comprehensive ICT solutions provider serving individuals and organizations across South Africa.

Our mission is to empower South African enterprises with uncapped, unshaped, and guaranteed high-speed connectivity that drives digital transformation and business growth.
